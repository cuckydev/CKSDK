Sets GPU framebuffers.

Parameters
wWidth of framebuffers
hHeight of framebuffers
oxX draw offset
oyY draw offset
x0X coordinate of framebuffer 0 in VRAM
y0Y coordinate of framebuffer 0 in VRAM
x1X coordinate of framebuffer 1 in VRAM
y1Y coordinate of framebuffer 1 in VRAM

Valid widths are 256, 320, 368, 512, and 640

If height exceeds 256, the GPU will be set to interlaced mode

Changes will apply on the next call to Flip()
